Primary timings affect RAM performance and system stability. When configuring RAM, optimizing these timings is crucial for better performance.
Values are: tRCD - tRP - tRAS (tRC = tRP + tRAS)

0 : Do Not Adjust (2400Mhz: 12 - 12 - 28) (CUST_ADJ only)
1 : 18 - 18 - 42 (Default timing)
2 : 17 - 17 - 39
3 : 16 - 16 - 36
4 : 15 - 15 - 34
5 : 14 - 14 - 32
6 : 13 - 13 - 30

The higher - the better, but might affect stability.
